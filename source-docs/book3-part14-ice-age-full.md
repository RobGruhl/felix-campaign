# Book 3, Part 14: Full Text (Chapters 108-114)

Chapter 108: …And Wyverns
o
The party travels with not much issue, until they reach the cliff under the city of
Bornir.
o
The party travels up the cliff on mountain goats and are attacked by wyverns
playing dead on the side of the cliff.
o
Armando and Goom take down one wyvern while protecting the dwarves.
o
A wyvern grabs the goat with Bromdir and Jhornos, and nearly drops them to
their doom.
o
Bromdir and Jhornos barely hold on, Jhornos grabs the blade of his sword to give
Bromdir a handhold.
o
Indigo summons giant owls, which they mount and defeat the wyvern in an aerial
battle.
o
The party flies on giant owls over the city of Bornir, heading straight for Thoros,
the dwarven capital.
-
Chapter 109: When there’s a will, there are woolly mammoths.
o
Using Indigo’s beast summoning powers, the party travels from mountain to
mountain, eventually landing on one too high to surpass
o
On foot, they follow a herd of mammoth to a wellspring of white library fluid,
like from the bowls and the Multi Beast’s grotto.
o
The party uses their dwarf workforce to break through the ice in their path.
o
The party encounters two frost giants, Orok and Dorok training in the
mountainside.
o
After convincing the giants they are against the king beneath the mountains and
slavery, the giants bring them to their home in Giantcleft.
o
In the giant village, weapons master Korloth attacks Jhornos, Aylan, Indigo, and
Bromdir without warning to test their mettle.
o
After being defeated by the party, he offers to train their bodies to be like iron.
o
The party undergoes one week of harsh training, becoming stronger.
o
The party meets Eastcliff, formerly the “peacekeeper king” of Bornir, but
managed to remove his upgrade through sheer force of will. He plans to take back
his kingdom from a mysterious usurper.
o
Eastcliff met the king beneath the mountains, he describes a beholder, a terrible
and powerful creature from the under dark that can manifest its dreams into
reality.
-
Chapter 110: Denver Scrubs: Snake Fucker
o
The party meets a powerful barbarian who uses power learned from Multibeast
named Denver Scrubs.
o
The party is informed that to access the frost giant’s most unique ability, runic
enchanting, they must complete the ritual of hunting a titan mammoth in the
giant’s dangerous tundra, patrolled by the legendary snow serpent.
o
Indigo and Denver use animals to scout the region, notably there is a strange
patch of magic ice, ice zombies, dire wolves, and a mysterious pale figure that
glowed.
o
The party decides to scout out the tundra. After a few miles, they feel the ground
rumbling. They remember hearing that when this happens, run, so they do.
o
They are not fast enough, and the snow begins to fall beneath them, and they land
in a tunnel system in the ice, with the serpent drawing closer.
o
Denver Scrubs trips, and the serpent catches up to her.
o
Denver uses her knowledge of beasts to perform a seductive dance to entrance the
serpent.
o
The serpent uses its frost breath and freezes her to death.
o
The party will not escape in time, so Indigo plants her seed of the Leshy, which
grows a huge plant that reaches the sky. They grab on.
o
The plant keeps growing, and they are hundreds of feet in the air with no way
down.
o
The frost serpent attacks the base, and the plant begins to tip over.
o
The party attempts to use leaves to glide down, to varying success.
o
Bromdir rescues Indigo from plummeting to her doom, and the party narrowly
escapes to the village.
-
Chapter 111: Drunk and Disorderly
o
After facing death, Goom lays down with wife while Bromdir and Indigo drink at
the giant’s tavern.
o
Frost giant drinks are especially strong, and Bromdir and Indigo get trashed.
o
They visit the magic patch of ice while intoxicated and find out from a giant that
it is a pool of scrying, and it will show you who you care about the most, whether
positive or negative.
o
Bromdir sees his wife, and she is sick, but she is also coughing up black sand
now.
o
Indigo sees her daughter, she is with Jasper, her long-lost lover.
o
Bromdir fights some zombies, explores an old dwarven fort with Indigo.
-
Chapter 112: Cyan’s Trick
o
That night, they are visited by Indigo’s daughter, who runs into the wilderness.
Bromdir and Indigo follow, thinking it to be the real thing.
o
They fall through some snow into a snowy part of the Fey realm.
o
They see a cabin in the woods, inside there is nice food and a nice old lady and
some boys.
o
Cyan appears, Indigo’s evil father, riding a fiery steed. He reveals that Indigo’s
daughter was a shapeshifter in disguise meant to lure Indigo to the cabin forever.
o
Cyan attempts to hurt Bromdir, engaging the party in combat. They manage to
knock him unconscious.
o
The nice old lady and boys reveal themselves to be fey creatures in Cyan’s thrall,
a hag and some nilbogs. They defeat them but catch the cabin on fire in the
process.
o
Bromdir tries to get last minute loot while the house is in flames. He finds golden
armor, a book on druidic practices by Cyan, and fancy cigars.
o
They leave on the fiery horse; it knows Indigo from childhood. They tie Cyan to
the back.
-
Chapter 113: Rupert Penis’ Trick
o
Lost in the Fey, Bromdir and Indigo wander.
o
They see a light, a whimsical Fey tavern built into the side of a tree.
o
There are many whacky looking characters inside, they talk to a blue caterpillar
with a deck of cards.
o
He says his name is Rupert Penis, and he will lead them out of the fey if they
bargain Bromdir’s name.
o
He refuses, and bargains Cyan’s name, doing Weekend at Bernie’s with his
unconscious body to manufacture consent.
o
They lose the hand of Blackjack, and Cyan becomes Rupert Penis and vice versa.
o
Cyan offers to gamble for Bromdir’s name, he refuses, and offers Rupert Penis’
cigars, save for the best one.
o
Cyan accepts, and defeats Bromdir again.
o
Cyan offers to wager three seeds of the Leshy, and a priceless magic jewel as well
as the way out, for Bromdir.
o
Bromdir accepts and loses. Cyan becomes Bromdir Cyan Sparrowcap, Bromdir
becomes “Sweetie Pie,” an affectionate nickname given to him by Bromdir Cyan
Sparrowcap.
o
Having gotten what he wanted, and feeling bad for Sweetie Pie, Bromdir leads
him out.
o
Sweetie Pie bargains desperately but does not get his name back.
-
Chapter 114: Final Preparations
o
The party investigates the pool of scrying after being told of its existence.
o
Jhornos sees his dad, Indigo sees her mom this time, standing in a room filled
with shortcuts leading all over Algrotha. Goom sees his childhood caretaker.
Armando sees the Clown Messiah, his most hated person, but all he sees is a room
full of dancing clowns, including one that looks strangely familiar, carrying a
scroll.
o
The party decides to leave the giants, deeming the hunt too dangerous.
o
Sweetie Pie sends a care package to his wife on a baby roc.
o
The giants escort the party to the edge of the mountain, as far as they’ll go.
Apparently, the bronze eye at the top of the ruin ringed mountain can incinerate
any visible target with ease.
o
Indigo paints the party white so they can sneak down the side of the mountain.

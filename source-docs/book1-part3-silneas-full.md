# Book 1, Part 3: Full Text (Chapters 20-27)

Chapter 20: The Gnome Forest
o
The party enters the gnome forest, knocks on the door of a random gnome’s
house, and asks for directions to Silneas’ tomb.
o
They find the house and go inside to find nothing except a locked door in the back
of the house which Viz picks. The door opens to a staircase leading below.
-
Chapter 21: Freaky Painters
o
The party finds themselves in a corridor with many doors, they open one leading
into a room full of painting supplies and eight painters. They are very gaunt
looking and only know to make paintings and serve Silneas. This confuses the
party as they all assumed he was dead.
o
Armando pretends to be a health inspector and rounds everyone up into the
garden. The garden is filled with many nice shrubs and terrifying stone statues.
o
The party heads to an office on this floor and finds a damaged map detailing the
western continent with several locations highlighted. They also find a box for a
ring which catches Eurostras’ attention. Finally, they find a doll of a purple jester,
and a silver compass.
o
The party heads back into the garden and tells everyone to stay put until they
come back. They learn about their boss, the custodian, and head down to another
floor to find him.
-
Chapter 22: The Custodian
o
The party enters a room with a scholarly man known as the custodian.
o
They see a painting of the ring of truth, Eurostras hides the ring of truth.
o
There is a door that’s locked from the outside, and another door with runes similar
to Gimel’s.
o
The custodian finds out about the ring and runs away. Gimel opens the door with
runes on it.
o
The party descends into a tall room filled with paintings of Silneas. The painting
in the center shows Silneas clutching the purple jester doll.
o
They also see the torso of Zayin, a golem similar to Gimel who holds resentment
towards Gimel for being a traitor.
o
Zayin explains that the paintings are to preserve Silneas’ mind, which is
somewhat intact.
o
Silneas found the library and is the only known person to have returned from it,
but his mind is not in a condition where it can exist normally.
o
Silneas needs the ring of truth to restore himself to sanity, which can be found at
the mountain of truth, there is also rumors of a fountain there which can restore
memories and a “sword of truth.”
o
Also, the mountain is at true North.
-
Chapter 23: Holy Fuck it’s a Basilisk.
o
A basilisk emerges from the central painting, Zayin orders it to take the ring from
Eurostras.
o
The gang fights the basilisk, the custodian, and several pale, mindwiped people
enter, some get turned to stone by the basilisk. Eurostras gets knocked out and
poisoned, Armando takes the ring and fights off the assailants. Viz throws a
dagger into the basilisk’s eye, and Corpium kills it. The assailants are driven away
by Armando.
o
The party decides to enter the Silneas painting, taking Zayin’s head with them.
-
Chapter 24: An Unpleasant Stroll Down Memory Lane
o
The party enters a throne room at the top of a tower, it’s snowing.
o
The party goes downstairs, they find an armory and a purple jester chained to the
wall, much like the doll they have.
o
The jester is sad because he will be executed soon for making fun of king Gilrand
III the Golden. He has a close relationship with Silneas Silvertongue.
o
They discover a dusty black chrysalis about three feet long.
o
The party descends into a kitchen/dining room, where people are celebrating.
There is another black chrysalis, but no one is acknowledging it.
o
The guards confirm the jester will be executed soon.
o
The party descends into a familiar hallway, and realize they are in the Silver
Tower, in the past? They came here before to find the staff of Silneas
Silvertongue. It is filled with chrysalises.
o
The gang relaxes in the tower library and share tales from the past.
o
Eurostras and Him learn to communicate via paper and pencil.
o
Armando tells the story of being accosted by clowns who worshipped the clown
messiah, and how Rizzmodeus was the only higher power that answered his
prayers.
o
Viz tells the story of their cult-like upbringing.
o
Corpium reveals how his adoptive brother Whortheus died.
o
Corpium finds out it was Eurostras’ fault, they almost come to blows, but resolve
their beef.
o
They run into Silneas’ mom, the acting lady of the Silvertongue family. She talks
about her son’s resentment towards wizards.
o
They follow her to the basement.
o
They solve the mirror puzzle from before, but in reverse.
o
They activate the portal, and step through to find themselves in a claustrophobic
room coated in chrysalises, with a painting of a black desert.
o
Someone steps on one by mistake, and a half-formed librarian is inside. It
explains that it is here to destroy this place.
o
It flies out.
o
The party enters the black desert painting.
-
Chapter 25: The Wizards Tomb
o
The party appears to be in the black desert from Eurostras’ dream. The only thing
they see for miles is a stone coffin.
o
They cautiously approach the coffin they can hear scratching within.
o
They open the lid, and find Silneas alive, looking pale and insane.
o
He says cryptic things about “The Library,” and how there is a shadow over
Grepping.
o
He sees the ring and regains lucidity for a moment to desperately lunge for it.
o
The party shuts the lid and leaves.
-
Chapter 26: Remember to Escape
o
The party takes a rest in the tower library, there’s more chrysalises, and some have
started to wriggle.
o
They wake up to librarians swarming the tower, ripping things off the walls.
o
The party kills a couple as they escape the painting.
-
Chapter 27: The Journey North
o
The party considers taking Zayin’s head with them but decides to leave it at the
tomb so it can instruct the painters and preserve Silneas’ mind.
o
They promote painter 1 into the next custodian.
o
The gang resolves to travel north and find the mountain to discover the mystery of
the library and achieve their personal goals.

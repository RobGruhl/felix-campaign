# Book 2, Part 9: Full Text (Chapters 73-80)

Chapter 73: Holy Shit
o
Armando and the Furious Four arrive in Soapstone, a magical holy city filled with
cathedrals and real angels.
o
They find out that it is the second day of the Peeking Festival, a seven-day festival
that happens once every seven years when the Grand Celestial (God) visits
Soapstone.
o
Armando summons Scroty in front of Justice, the largest angel. He manages to
convince her to let him keep Scroty around.
o
The party heads to the market cathedral to cash in their gold from the skeleton
dungeon.
-
Chapter 74: Value
o
The party meets the economic angel, a 20-foot angel with a golden mask.
o
She can determine the exact value of an item and is willing to buy and sell.
o
The party sells their gold for 8000 gold pieces.
o
The party buys religious garb and an immovable rod and potions.
o
The party asks about things to do in Soapstone, the economic angel tells them
about the dark cathedral in the dead zone, where the angels cannot set foot.
-
Chapter 75: The Dead Zone
o
The party heads to the forest surrounding the dark cathedral.
o
Indigo talks to the trees to figure out what the hell is going on. They can only
remember the past day.
o
The trees tell her that a man with a big bag passed recently.
o
They reach the entrance, there are two gargoyle statues. Inside is a run-down
church room.
o
The party finds a way up on the balcony. They discover a well-kept but dark room
inside.
-
Chapter 76: Da House
o
They find a few well-kept rooms, and a stone room with strange formations and a
seemingly bottomless hole.
o
They hear a sweeping sound.
o
They find a shadow that is sweeping.
o
Five shadows ambush the party while they talk to the sweeping one.
o
The shadows drain the party’s strength, nearly killing Armando.
o
Aylan uses turn undead and instantly destroys all of them.
o
Indigo marks the rooms they have already explored with paint.
o
Bromdir discovers a valuable looking golden cat with emerald eyes and a very
evil aura. Jhornos puts it in the bag of holding.
-
Chapter 77: Da Rooms
o
They find a window and see that the land below appears to be partially black
desert and partially Soapstone.
o
They go up a tower and find another weird stone room.
o
They find a living room that has been used recently.
o
They find a librarian bowl filled with decorative wooden balls.
o
They find a pool of oily liquid.
o
They find a coffin, Jhornos realizes they might be dealing with a vampire.
o
Armando and the Furious Four arrive at the top of the tower, in a room with a big
window that overlooks an entirely black desert. There is a pile of gifts in the
middle of the room. A well-dressed man with a cape looks out of the window.
-
Chapter 78: Da Host
o
The man introduces himself as the vampire Magis and threatens the party.
o
The party insists they mean no ill will and are just looking for the presents, they
ask Magis why he took them.
o
Magis tells the party there is no way mortals will understand what he his doing,
but they do, because it’s the library.
o
The party finds out that Aylan knows about the library.
o
Magis mentions that he was friends with Silneas Silvertongue. The party says
they met him, and that he’s still alive.
o
The party offers to help Magis look for the gift of interest in exchange for Magis
returning the gifts.
o
The party finds the dark purple crystalline stone marked with the rune “Nun.”
-
Chapter 79: GIMEL
o
The party summons Gimel to see if he knows what’s up.
o
Entranced by the stone, Gimel approaches it and puts it inside himself.
o
The party, interested, lets him do this.
o
Gimel changes when he puts Nun inside of his compartment, growing 20 feet tall
and platinum with four arms and a 360-degree rotating torso.
o
Gimel starts to move, Bromdir approaches Gimel and yells his name, Gimel blasts
him with a laser, nearly killing him. Jhornos gives him a healing potion.
o
Gimel says the word “North,” repeatedly, and that Silneas Silvertongue is not his
master.
o
The party struggles to hold back the overpowered Gimel, he heats up his body and
melts through the stone of the floor.
o
Aylan successfully uses “Hold Person” on Gimel, Bromdir loots Magis’ safe and
takes from it “The Traveler’s Guide to Larithia.” The party gathers the gifts and
prepares to retreat.
o
They run out of the dead zone to seek help from the angels.
-
Chapter 80: Angels X Machina
o
Aylan tells Justice that something bad will be emerging from the north of the dead
zone, and to prepare an army of angels.
o
Jhornos convinces the economic angel to lend him the dragon armor.
o
Gimel emerges and is fired upon by the party and Justice plus twenty angels.
o
The angels go blind and bleed black goo from their eyes when looking at Gimel.
Even Justice, who wears a blindfold, has her vision impaired by this.
o
Destroys a house, before being destroyed by Jhornos’ magic missile and
collapsing into the dreidel and Nun on the ground.
o
Aylan heals the angels.
o
Nun corrupts civilians into doing its bidding, and Armando tackles a vicar and a
child.
o
Aylan wrangles Nun into the dead zone, and into the dark cathedral.
o
Magis comes down and is uncomfortable with the decision to bring the sinister
orb back.

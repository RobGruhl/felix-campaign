# Felix Campaign - Source Documentation

## Campaign Overview

**The Felix Campaign** is an epic D&D adventure spanning 141 chapters across 4 books and 16 parts. The story follows a rotating cast of adventurers as they uncover the mysteries of The Library - a dimension of forbidden knowledge guarded by skeletal creatures - and pursue the Ring of Truth to the Mountain of Truth in the far north.

The campaign begins with a simple dungeon crawl that spirals into cosmic horror, political intrigue, and personal tragedy. Along the way, the party encounters three imprisoned demon lords, a beholder king who manifests dreams into reality, and the lingering influence of Ilrune VII, the ancient Dreamer-King who sealed away great evils.

**Key Themes:** Memory and identity, the cost of forbidden knowledge, sacrifice, found family, the corruption of power

## Recurring NPCs

### The Wizard Council
- **Ryan** - Council member who hires the party; mind destroyed by Nun, becomes "Smoky the Clown"
- **Krenko** - Council member; mind destroyed by Nun
- **Greenwise** - Council member, dealer of weed, grows magical forests; last sane member
- **Aquarius the Hidden** - Final wizard council member, hidden in the north, specializes in enchanting
- **Skithiryx the Laughing Wizard** - Betrayer who stole Nun from the blue pyramid

### Demon Lords (The Three Humors)
- **Rizzmodeus** - Demon lord of fire, Armando's patron; freed by Corpium
- **Midas** - Demon lord of greed; offers devil's bargains through golden items
- **Dionysus** - Demon lord of revelry; sealed beneath the Glass Citadel

### Other Key NPCs
- **Godrick the Golden** - Wizard with the Staff of Silneas, at war with Mimeoplasm
- **Silneas Silvertongue** - Legendary wizard, only person to return from The Library, alive but insane
- **Gimel/Shin** - Golden golem from dreidel, four personalities including Nun (stores wizard knowledge)
- **Khovum Pamarath** - Triton ship captain and collector
- **Ilrune VII** - The Dreamer-King who sealed the demons; appears through the Ring of Truth
- **Chromium** - Beholder, "King Beneath the Mountains," manifests dreams into reality
- **The Multibeast** - Forest spirit with empathy, appears as different animals
- **Magis** - Vampire, former friend of Silneas, lives in Soapstone's Dead Zone

# Book 2, Part 7: Full Text (Chapters 58-65)

Chapter 58: Armando’s Return
o
When Armando switches places with Rizzmodeus he wakes up in the Glass
Abyss.
o
Armando learns from a voice claiming to be Dionysus about what’s going on, and
it asks him to travel to the glass citadel when he is freed and release Dionysus.
Dionysus tells Armando that Midas, himself, and Rizzmodeus were the three
humors of the ancient city, and they were locked away by Ilrune VII, The
Dreamer-King.
o
Armando wakes up in an inn owned by Bromdir Sparrowcap. He has half a cream
pie, some beer, and a note from Rizzmodeus politely offering him the eyes of
Rizzmodeus, items used to locate people.
o
Armando sees a prismatic blue pyramid in the burned remains of HQ.
o
Armando sleeps and has a horrible dream where a hanged purple jester is inside of
the blue pyramid in a black desert, and his body is distorted and unnatural. It
extends its arm toward Armando through the pyramid and he wakes up.
o
Armando uses the eye of Rizzmodeus locked on to Corpium to find the party, on
the way he runs into Mary of the clown, a clown messiah loyalist who used to live
in the HQ forest. Armando selflessly offers to take her with him, despite her
clown leanings.
o
Armando meets the party outside of the glass citadel, and discovers that a lot has
changed, and that his dear friend Eurostras has died. He meets Indigo and
Jhornos, two new allies.
-
Chapter 59: To Find the Wibex
o
Rizzmodeus gives Armando his promised boon, a red sword that can be turned
into a sword of fire by plunging it into the heart of a Wibex.
o
Jhornos explains that a Wibex is a creature of cruelty born when a pregnant goat
dies in a forest fire. They are drawn to intense suffering.
o
Rizzmodeus says that a Wibex was spotted near the Glass Citadel.
o
Corpium tries to steal all the beads of teleportation, alienating the vendor.
o
The party sets off into the woods, and quickly realize that travelling in the woods
by night gets them supernaturally lost in “The Deep Forest.”
-
Chapter 60: The Deep Forest
o
A faerie offers to lead the party to someone who suffers a lot, her old friend.
o
She leads them past many wondrous things, including an arch with elvish writing
on it.
o
They arrive at the house of Rickard Gray, in a clearing surrounded by tall trees
and yellow mushrooms.
o
Rickard hates the faerie, who flees, laughing. He is a donkey man who was
originally human and got lost on the way to his wedding.
o
He tells the party to never fall asleep in the Deep Forest.
o
Rickard claims to be severely allergic to magic and asks the party to leave their
magic items outside.
o
The party puts the ring of truth on him, and he regains the appearance of a man,
but then he freaks out when he realizes it is magic.
o
The party realizes the agony Rickard experiences when seeing magic could attract
the Wibex, so they convince him to stand outside.
o
It works, Rickard is brought outside and the Wibex arrives, a huge serpentine
creature that bleats menacingly.
-
Chapter 61: Goat Simulator
o
The fight against the Wibex starts well, they whale on it and seemingly defeat it
before it reemerges, engulfed in painful flame.
o
The Wibex knocks a tree over, Indigo talks to the trees and recruits them to the
party’s side.
o
The Wibex fights fiercely, knocking Corpium unconscious.
o
Indigo revives Corpium, who is now furious.
o
Corpium charges back into the Wibex, injuring himself severely and falling
unconscious again.
o
Jhornos cuts the Wibex’ head off, Armando stabs its heart with his sword and
pulls it out, it is engulfed in magic flame.
o
Corpium is not breathing, he has died due to injuries sustained in battle.
-
Chapter 62: Corpium is Never Coming Back
o
As Corpium dies, Rizzmodeus asks him to pass on his pact to a willing heir, or
have it be nullified. He chooses to pass it on to Jhornos before his soul is dragged
violently towards the sea near the Glass Citadel.
o
The party leaves Corpium’s corpse in the house with Rickard, Patricia I was
buried, Jhornos takes Patricia II and bonds to it.
o
The party is now lost in the Deep Forest.
-
Chapter 63: Lost
o
They encounter the faerie again and beg for a way out.
o
She leads them to the stone arch, it is a “shortcut.”
o
The shortcut is a pathway through space, the party travels through it and
encounters animals travelling between forests. They slowly become scarier as
time goes on, an elephant warns Indigo not to stay to long in a shortcut.
o
They end up deep in the dry forest, Scroty flies up to confirm. Their navigation is
still unreliable.
o
Indigo talks to a fox, who offers to lead them to someone who can help.
o
They are lead past the inexplicable wonders of the Deep Forest and past many
huge unknown Fae creatures.
-
Chapter 64: The Multibeast
o
The party finds a grotto where an ape bathes in milky library water. He has the
same scar and sword as Eros’ chimp.
o
He explains that he is the Multibeast, one of the few spirits of the forest with
empathy. He was the giant bear who warned the party of the forest earlier.
o
He implores the party to join him in the pool, but they are damaged by the water.
o
He asks Indigo if she truly wants to become familiar with the forest, and if she
does, she should drink from the water.
o
She decides what she wants to, and drinks from the water, affecting her the same
way the water from the bowl affected others. A librarian head appears in the pool,
cursing Indigo with knowledge of the library.
o
Shibu the white fox leads the party out of the forest.
-
Chapter 65: Grief and Alchemy
o
The party grieves at the Inn. They dream of the jester in the pyramid.
o
They meet Bromdir, the owner of the inn, who shares the knowledge of the
library.
o
The party, with Bromdir, travels to the house of the outskirts’ alchemist, avoiding
Coal burg as it has been taken over by the Mimeoplasm forces.
o
The door is open, the party explores the alchemist’s basement and meets his
transparent homunculi.
o
They discover a setup where a pale frog is being pumped full of potion. They are
confronted by an ice and a fire elemental. They ask them where to find the
alchemist.
o
They find the alchemist through a room that is choked with black smog, billowing
out of one of the potion vats.
o
The alchemist explains that he is brewing a potion, trying to reach perfection,
under the guidance of “The great alchemist.”
o
Jhornos and Armando tank the toxins to open the vents and release the smog,
Jhornos falls into a vat of healing potion, which is okay!
o
The outskirts alchemist trades for a mushroom from the Deep Forest, adding it to
the brew and drinking from it.
o
The party earns their sleeping potion.
o
The Alchemist his tumors develop into an extra set of arms, split at the elbow,
they are made of librarian material.
o
The Alchemist demonstrates his perfect control over the physical world by
shattering a flask into perfect triangles.
o
The frog threatens to escape from the tube, but with a careful touch The
Alchemist repairs it.
o
The party is weirded out and leaves, heading back to the inn.

# Book 3, Part 11: Full Text (Chapters 85-90)

Chapter 85: A Bug’s Life
o
The party heads off into the desert where after a few days of travel, come across a
strange oasis.
o
In the oasis they find some magical fruit but before they can pick any, they are
stopped by a small army of bug-like creatures.
o
The bugs explain that the fruit is sacred to them and that the party cannot have it.
They say that they can have a fruit if the party helps slay the racoon that is taking
their fruit.
o
The party agrees and sets a trap with one of the fruits as bait. They snare the
racoon, and it frees itself fast but is killed by Bromdir.
o
The bugs rejoice and give the party an option of which fruit to have as their
reward. They choose the apple of strength.
o
The party discovers the oasis stems from the remains of a dead dragon. Jhornos
finds the skull and takes the teeth.
-
Chapter 86: Axe Upgrades
o
The party sees an incoming storm in the desert and shelters in the ruin of a tower
nearby.
o
During the storm, Jhornos uses Patricia II as a lightning rod in order to make it
stronger.
o
The party hears multiple roars and saw something flying in the sky.
o
The party sleeps through the rest of the storm and its uneventful.

Chapter 87: Eye-Guy
o
The party arrives at the sand-swept mountains and begins to make the trek
towards the ruins of Myon Thalas.
o
The party encounters a cyclops, they do battle.
o
Indigo summons a powerful boar, Bromdir climbs the cyclops and stabs its eye.
Jhornos slices it in half and steals the pickaxes from its necklace.
-
Chapter 88: Gifts From a Demon Lord
o
Armando finds a red snake who leads him behind a tree, then swaps with
Rizzmodeus.
o
Rizzmodeus offers to upgrade Scroty to have fire breath in exchange for his lame
gift from the holy man. He does.
o
Jhornos is eavesdropping.
o
Rizzmodeus tells Armando that part of his long-term plan is to march his troops
into the Worldscar and take over Hel from the Demogorgon.
o
Rizzmodeus calls for him and the party to renegotiate the deal.
o
Rizzmodeus updates Jhornos and Armando that things have gotten worse,
Mimeoplasm is being advised by someone called The Alchemist, a powerful
figure who has twisted the Mimeoplasm’s troops into nightmarish abominations.
Spies have taken advantage of his pact with Corpium and know they cannot be
harmed in the citadel.
o
Rizzmodeus asks Jhornos to renegotiate on Corpium’s behalf.
o
Jhornos agrees, letting him bring harm to the citizens if there is reasonable
suspicion, they are spies on the condition that he tell him where to find a new
sword.
o
Rizzmodeus knows of a sword at the bottom of Wish Lake.
-
Chapter 89: The Undying City
o
The party enters a petrified forest.
o
They encounter Sir Goom, a heroic and powerful paladin who knows of the
library and intends on helping the Dwarflands.
o
They see a defunct shortcut in the forest.
o
They see a mummy pushing a wheelbarrow with three tied up dwarves in it.
o
Bromdir cuts the mummy’s legs off and saves the dwarves, Murin, Morim, and
Kod. Kod isn’t very bright, but he hits it off with Bromdir.
o
The mummy needed to sacrifice the dwarves for a ritual, claiming that death
would come for Myon-Thalas otherwise.
o
The party arrives at the city, to find a horde of mummies and three crucified
mummies in pain. They don’t seem to mind Bromdir’s mild torture of them. They
explain that they are The Penitent, and they suffer for the sins of their people.
o
The party tries to free them, the mummy horde attacks.
o
Armando uses the spell fireball, incinerating the horde nearly instantly.
o
The party enters the palace.
-
Chapter 90: Death Will Come to You and Your People
o
The party finds a room of bugs and a room of plants.
o
Jhornos does pushups with Bromdir, Indigo uses poison spray to exterminate the
bugs.
o
In the room of plants, grey oozes attack. The chimp blade is ruined.
o
They find a room of eerie red cloth dancers, they fight them, but the dancers are
sneaky and flee upstairs.
o
Upstairs, they meet the queen, who has preserved herself perfectly. She alludes to
being connected with Larithia.
o
She almost makes a deal with the party and mentions that she made a deal with
Magis after he took her book, but the party refuses to let sacrifice take place and
fakes her out, engaging her in battle.
o
She is a powerful magic user but is defeated by Sir Goom via a spear through the
head, pinning her to the wall.
o
Bromdir steals a ton of loot.
o
The party rests in the palace.
